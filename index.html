Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

Payments are securely processed through our platform. You can cash out anytime after completing a task and the requester has approved your work. We offer direct bank deposits, PayPal transfers, or QuickTasker debit card options for instant access to your earnings.

We charge a 15% service fee on each completed task. This covers payment processing, customer support, liability insurance, and platform maintenance. For example, if you earn $100 for a task, you'll receive $85 after our fee.

You can start earning immediately after completing your profile and passing our identity verification (takes 1-2 business days). Many taskers complete their first task within hours of signing up!

No, signing up and browsing tasks is completely free. We only earn when you earn - our fee is taken as a percentage of completed tasks. We do offer optional Premium Memberships ($9.99/month) that give you early access to high-paying tasks and lower service fees.

Our customer support team is available 24/7 to help resolve any issues. Payments are held in escrow until work is approved, and we offer mediation services if there's a disagreement between tasker and requester.
